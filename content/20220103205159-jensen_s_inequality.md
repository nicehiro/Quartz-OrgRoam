If \\(X\\) is a random variable and \\(\phi\\) is a [convex function]({{< relref "20220104195751-convex_function.md" >}}), then

\\[
\mathbb{E}[\phi(X)] \geq \phi(\mathbb{E}(X))
\\]

See the simple proof on ROAM_REF.

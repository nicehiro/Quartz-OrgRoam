Neural Fields is a mapping from spatial coordinates to scene properties, such as occupancy, color, radiance, etc, but unlike [Voxel Grids]({{< relref "20240709203658-voxel_grids.md" >}}) - in which the mapping is from a discrete cell to the value at that voxel - in a neural filed the mapping is a learned function, typically a multi-layer perceptron.

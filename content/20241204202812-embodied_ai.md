## Foundation Models Applications {#foundation-models-applications}


### Low-Level Perception {#low-level-perception}

Representative studies utilizing foundation models, such as CLIPort and REFLECT, to <span class="underline">extract semantic information from images, texts and spatial information</span>.


### High-Level Perception {#high-level-perception}

Foundation models for high-level perception involve the <span class="underline">transformation and utilization of results obtained from low-level perception into forms such as maps, rewards, and motion constrains</span>.


### High-Level Planning {#high-level-planning}

Foundation models for high-level planning execute higher-level <span class="underline">abstract task planning</span>, excluding direct control.


### Low-Level Planning {#low-level-planning}

Foundation models for low-level planning execute low-level motion control, including <span class="underline">joint and end effector control</span>.


### Data Augmentation {#data-augmentation}

Foundation models augment dataset by <span class="underline">changing background, adding distractors and altering object textures to improve the robustness of the learning</span>.

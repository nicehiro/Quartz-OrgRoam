-   **Calvin**: CALVIN: A Benchmark for Language-Conditioned Policy Learning for Long-Horizon Robot Manipulation Tasks. PyBullet &amp; Franka. 2022. [[Paper](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2112.03227.pdf)] [[Website](http://calvin.cs.uni-freiburg.de)] [[Code](https://github.com/mees/calvin)]

> The dataset itself contains short (average length is around 60 steps, and one language instruction) trajectories, while the evaluation strategy contains long-horizon multi-task settings, with multiple instruction involved in one task.

-   **LoHoRavens**: LoHoRavens: A Long-Horizon Language-Conditioned Benchmark for Robotic Tabletop Manipulation. PyBullet &amp; UR5. 2023. [[Paper](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2310.12020)] [[Website](https://cisnlp.github.io/lohoravens-webpage/)] [[Code](https://github.com/Shengqiang-Zhang/LoHo-Ravens)]

-   **Mujoco Manipulus**: MUJOCO MANIPULUS: A ROBOT LEARNING BENCHMARK FOR GENERALIZABLE TOOL MANIPULATION. Mujoco. 2025. [[Paper](https://openreview.net/pdf/e2313c7cd51006c7d28687cf861e963827282bb2.pdf?utm_source=chatgpt.com)]
